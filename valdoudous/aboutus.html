About Us

Valérie Umiglia's passion has always been knitting. She has created many different things in her life, like hats, sweaters, scarfs, or even blankets! In 2009, her son's half-sister had just been born, and he wanted the perfect gift for the newest member in the family. He didn't have much money, so he decided to ask her if there was anything that she could do for him. Right then and there the idea was born for Val Doudous (in French, doudou means lovie). Her project became more and more known in her friend group, as many people started to want to buy them as gifts. In 2021, after over 10 years of refining her ideas for those lovies, she decided to finally take a step forward and start actually putting more time into it. She now offers over 70 different colors for those lovies. She loves creating those and loves seeing the joy that those lovies bring to the families around her.

Frequently Asked Questions:
How can I order a Doudou?
All our orders are processed through email. You can email us at valdoudous@gmail.com. Please tell us the name of the color you want(found under the picture) and your address.

How big are they?
The height is about 33cm (approx. 13in), and the width is about 28cm (approx. 11in)

What are they made of?
The outside is made of 100% cotton, and the inside of the head is stuffed with 100% polyester batting.

How can I wash them?
They are fully washable in the washing machine on low temperature

What colors are available?
All of the available colors are on the website's main page.

Can I place an order for a specific color?
Of course! We just ask for your patience, as it takes a lot of time for Valérie to create them. If you need one quickly, feel free to pick one from the main page. If you wanted something more specific, she would have to create it from scratch, which takes time. Depending on specificities, prices may vary.

Do you ship anywhere in the world?
Yes, prices will vary depending on the location though. For more information about prices for your location, feel free to contact us by email (valdoudou@gmail.com)
